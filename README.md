JUNGLE DASH — open in any browser and hit RUN! 🎮
Here's what's in the game:
Controls:

← →  or A D — switch lane
↑ / W / Space — jump
↓ / S — slide/duck
Swipe on mobile
Esc — pause

Gameplay systems:

🏃 3-lane endless runner — speed increases over time
❤️ 3 lives with invincibility frames after each hit (player flashes)
🏆 Score (distance-based) + coin bonuses
🌄 Milestone popup every 150m
💀 Game over screen with best score tracking

8 obstacle types:

Wall Left / Right — block 2 lanes, dodge to open lane
Wall Center — blocks middle only, go left or right
Hurdle — low bar across all lanes, must jump
Stone Arch — low ceiling across all lanes, must slide
Fire Columns Left / Right — fire traps blocking 2 lanes
Crystal Spires — crystal formations blocking one side
