<b><h1>JUNGLE DASH </h1><b>— open in any browser and hit RUN! 🎮<br>
Here's what's in the game:<br><br>
Controls:<br>
<br>
← →  or A D — switch lane<br>
↑ / W / Space — jump<br>
↓ / S — slide/duck<br>
Swipe on mobile<br>
Esc — pause<br>
<br>
Gameplay systems:<br>
<br>
🏃 3-lane endless runner — speed increases over time<br>
❤️ 3 lives with invincibility frames after each hit (player flashes)<br>
🏆 Score (distance-based) + coin bonuses<br>
🌄 Milestone popup every 150m<br>
💀 Game over screen with best score tracking<br>
<br>
8 obstacle types:
<br>
Wall Left / Right — block 2 lanes, dodge to open lane<br>
Wall Center — blocks middle only, go left or right<br>
Hurdle — low bar across all lanes, must jump<br>
Stone Arch — low ceiling across all lanes, must slide<br>
Fire Columns Left / Right — fire traps blocking 2 lanes<br>
Crystal Spires — crystal formations blocking one side<br>
